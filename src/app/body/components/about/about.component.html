Innovation Management Academy Albania

Innovation Management Academy Albania has become a recognized leader in intrapreneurship education and bridging the diverse ICT talents and hidden gems with corporates. It is divided in two main pillars: Innovation Academy & Talent Acquisition.

IMAAs’ Innovation Academy is a unique educational format that incorporates lectures, training, and workshops, aiming to build your organization’s capability to manage innovation. It's boot-camp will provide the participants with the opportunity to learn why they should innovate, what they could innovate, and then how to do that, from some of the best regional and international experts in that field. IMAA is mainly focused on innovation, digital transformation, operational excellence, growth strategies, talent development, coaching integration, switch carrier, and career guidance by bringing into light active stages programs and mentoring them by industry professionals.

IMAAs’ Talent Acquisition is to make the Albanian Market aware of the first unique platform in providing better access to new job opportunities in the ICT sector and innovative products/ services (intermediations process) though providing:

  • A talent acquisition matchmaking process (focusing on how a company intends to position itself as an attractive destination and how it plans to source, assess, employ, and bring on board the young generations to respond to the market employment needs in Albania in the ICT sector.
  • Providing soft skills training to fulfill the students' & ICT Companies' gaps and challenges in 12 soft skills modules.
